Our Story

Millions of people around the world still lack access to clean and safe drinking water. At Charity: Water, we believe access to clean water is a basic human right. Our mission is to bring sustainable water solutions to communities in need and create long-lasting change.

Real Impact: Meet Amina

Amina used to walk miles every day just to collect unsafe water. Now, thanks to your support, her village has a clean water well. She can attend school, stay healthy, and dream about her future.
